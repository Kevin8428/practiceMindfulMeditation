Beginner's Guide

Three great resources which are highly acclaimed and provide a great introduction to meditation and how to start practicing.

Mindfulness in Plain english

By Bhante Henepola Gunaratana

This is an increadibly powerful book for all practitioners, although it offers an amazing introduction into practice for beginners. Bhante Henepola Gunaratana is a Sri Lankan monk who's written more than a half dozen publications to date, all centered around medtation, mindfulness and happiness.

View

Headspace

A Meditation App for Phone and Computer

This is a gerat resource with dozens of programs specializing in stress, anxiety, sleep, happiness and more. The guided meditations are amazing for beginners and provide techniques which last long after you stop using the app. With a free trial it's definitely worth exploring.

View

Free Guided Meditation

From UCLA Mindful Awareness Research Center

This is another great resource in both English and Spanish. It has guided meditation as well as background music. It can be played from a computer or downloaded on iTunes for free.

Wherever You Go, There You Are

Mindfulness Meditation In Everyday Life

Celebrating more than 20 years in circulation, this book is a national best seller and for good reason. This book shows there's no secret to meditation; it can be done and appreciated by anyone. It is written by Jon Kabat-Zinn, a renowned researcher in the study of mindfulness-based stress reduction.
