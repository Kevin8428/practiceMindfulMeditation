Why was this page built?

The purpose of this site is to provide resources and introductory tips on how to begin mindfulness meditation. This site aims to highlight the benefits of mindfulness meditation and promote it as a daily practice to combat a multitude of mental health issues faced by all types of people on a daily basis. It’s affordable (or free) and is able to be practiced by anyone.

Mental health is a serious concern, and meditation can help.

Anxiety disorders are the most common mental illness in the U.S., affecting 40 million adults ages 18 and older. While highly treatable, roughtly two-thirds of those suffering do not receive treatment. Anxiety disorders cost the U.S. more than $42 billion a year. This is roughly one-third of the country’s $148 billion mental health bill.

Meditation makes people happier.
