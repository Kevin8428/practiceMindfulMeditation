Why was this page built?

The purpose of this site is to provide resources and introductory tips on how to begin mindfulness meditation. Mental health is a serious concern.

  • Anxiety disorders are the most common mental illness in the U.S., affecting 40 million adults ages 18 and older.
  • While highly treatable, roughly two-thirds of those suffering do not receive treatment.

And meditation can help.

  • It has been proven time and again to reduce anxiety and decrease stress.
  • It can help with depression.
  • It can improve your sleep.
  • It can help you focus.
