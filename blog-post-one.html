Alan Watts was many things: A philosopher, writer, priest, speaker and more.

The story of the Chinese farmer is a great parable shared by Watts and is a great listen for when things are bad (or good!)

If you'd like to know more, below is a great list of works by Watts:

  • The Wisdom of Insecurity: A Message for an Age of Anxiety
  • The Book: On the Taboo Against Knowing Who You Are
  • The Way of Zen
  • Become What You Are
  • This Is It: and Other Essays on Zen and Spiritual Experiences
